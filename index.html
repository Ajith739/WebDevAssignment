Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion Process

High-performance extruders with advanced screw design deliver uniform melt flow for consistent pipe wall thickness and dimensional accuracy.

  • Controlled melt temperature
  • High-output screw design

Controlled Cooling System

Multi-stage cooling tanks maintain optimal pipe geometry, ensuring dimensional stability and preventing deformation during solidification.

  • Water temperature control
  • Gradual cooling profile

Precision Sizing & Calibration

Vacuum calibration sleeves and sizing tanks maintain exact outer diameters across all pipe sizes to meet international standards.

  • Vacuum calibration technology
  • Tight dimensional tolerances

Rigorous Quality Control

  • 100% pressure testing
  • Dimensional verification
